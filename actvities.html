Activities

Our School at Madanpur Khadar

We have been running a primary school at Madanpur Khadar focussed to provide basic education to 100+ children of specially migrant families with a small group of teachers and well wishers spreading the light of knowledge in these young minds. Most of the students are from Bengali families and come from a harsh economic condition, aspiring to break free of social boundaies to be granted in govt. schools. We also provide them with books, stationaries uniforms, along with mentorship and guidance to help them shine bighter.

Health & Social Welfare Activities

We have been organising regular Medical Health Camps in various regions of Central Delhi to facilitate and help Senior Citizens over the years.
We have also been instrumental in facilitating educational schoarships awards to meritorious students from 8 bengali schools in Delhi. More over we are keen to help individuals contributing efforts in propagating Bengal's culture.

Cultural Events & Workshops

We celebrate the existence of our Praner Manush Kobiguru Rabindranath Thakur in our lives through Kobi Pronam, and Baish-e Srabon.
We celebrate Theatre festivals and Cine Utsav for movie and theatre buffs.
We get the best of Bangla books in Boi Mela along with several musical events. We also organize and clebrate Bijoya Sammelani, and our recent initiative Baul Mela was a great success.

Publication, Editorial & Literary Activities

Since the last 44 years, we have ben publishing and promoting our magazine, Digangan. It has received recognition from the National Book Trust as a "Little Magazine". We also promote and feature books from various expatriate writers and Bengali Authors..
